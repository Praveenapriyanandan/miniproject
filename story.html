Once upon a time, in a magical forest, there lived a kind bear named Benny. One sunny morning, Benny heard a small voice crying for help. He found a little rabbit stuck in a bush. Benny gently lifted the branches and helped the rabbit escape. The grateful rabbit thanked Benny and hopped away happily. Benny felt proud of helping his friend and continued his adventure, knowing that helping others is the best adventure of all.
